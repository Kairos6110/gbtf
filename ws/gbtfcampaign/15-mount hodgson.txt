### 1. SITUATION & MISSION

Sentinel's drone production capability is on its last legs following our successful strikes against its manufacturing and transportation network. With their production capacity crippled, we are now targeting the source of their raw materials. Mount Hodgson serves as Sentinel's primary supplier of rare-earth minerals used in drone electronics, batteries, and advanced components.

The mountainous industrial sector is heavily exploited for mining and on-site chemical refining before shipment. Our disruption of Sentinel's logistics has left large stockpiles trapped inland, forcing them to rapidly move valuable ore and refined materials to offshore buyers. Intelligence confirms the mines and processing facilities remain operational under heavy security.

During infiltration aboard HORUS 3, our contracted helicopter was engaged by a surface-to-air missile from an area previously assessed as clear by ISR. The aircraft crashed near Auroa's highest peak. The pilot was killed on impact. HUNTER survived with minor injuries but lost all weapons, communications equipment, and mission gear. With sub-zero temperatures and no contact with TOC, the team must move two kilometers downhill to Drone Station W041 to acquire enemy weapons, communications equipment, and gear. Once rearmed, HUNTER will re-establish communications, finish the mission, and proceed to the covert Resistance safe haven codenamed EREWHON for medical checks. The pilot provided us with exact GRID coordinates before the crash, we no longer possess the navigation or communications equipment required to precisely locate the rendezvous point until operational capability has been restored.

* **Phase I - Mount Hodgson Bivouac (Survival & Recovery):** Begin at the Mount Hodgson crash site (the bivouac), adjust at night. Your team has survived the helicopter shootdown but lost ALL mission equipment. You start with only your uniform and a combat knife—no firearms, ammunition, or radio communications. (KNIFE ONLY UNTIL EQUIPMENT IS REALISTICALLY ACQUIRED.) Move cautiously downhill toward the nearby Drone Station, silently eliminating isolated patrols whenever possible. Scavenge enemy equipment to gradually rebuild your combat capability, starting with basic unsuppressed sidearms. For the whole mission duration, expect only standard Sentinel-issued weapons with iron sights and minimal attachments since the enemy doesn't use Advanced SOPMOD style modifications. (Roleplay picking up weapons from fallen enemies but keep them barebones no attachments and silencers. No drone no binoculars, no items. The goal is to purposely make it as difficult 'SURVIVAL' experience). [PHASE CODE: ICEBOUND]
* **Phase II - Drone Station W041 (Asset & Radio Acquisition):** Infiltrate Drone Station W041 to acquire enemy weapons, communications equipment, and resume communication with TOC. [PHASE CODE: SIGNAL]
* **Phase III - Silent Valley Mine (Telemetry Sabotage & OP):** Conduct recon from an elevated OP above Silent Valley Mine. Infiltrate the mine's primary control station to upload custom malware into the automated excavation drills' guidance telemetry, rendering the machinery inoperable while preserving the structural integrity of the mine. [PHASE CODE: LANDSLIDE]
* **Phase IV - Chem-Extraction Complex (Refinery Sabotage):** Execute an assault on the heavily guarded Chem-Extraction Complex. Locate the hazardous chemical processing valves, override the automated safety locks, and trigger a controlled internal thermal overload to ruin the refinement machinery without causing an external environmental breach (hack terminals). [PHASE CODE: ACID RAIN]
* **Phase V - Bald Peak Mine (Surface Sabotage):** Infiltrate the surface facilities of **Bald Peak Mine**. Sabotage the primary ore crusher's electrical substation and override the overland conveyor control terminal to shut down processing operations, rendering the transport of raw minerals impossible while avoiding permanent structural damage, and liberate all civilian hostages working under duress. [PHASE CODE: GLACIER]
* **Phase VI - Erewhon (Final Exfiltration):** Move on foot to the covert resistance safe haven codenamed EREWHON for final exfiltration, debrief, and medical checks. [PHASE CODE: SAFEHOUSE]
