**OPORD NO. 7116**

**CALLSIGN:** HUNTER

### 1. SITUATION & MISSION

The mountainous, industrial sector of Mount Hodgson is being heavily exploited by Sentinel Corp to feed raw materials into their drone mass-production pipelines. Sentinel forces have secured the regional heavy mining grids to extract rare-earth elements required for drone thermal shielding and chassis armor. They are utilizing a specialized chemical processing footprint to refine these elements on-site before transport. HUNTER is ordered to execute an industrial interdiction operation to systematically sabotage the extraction infrastructure, seize logistics scheduling assets, and neutralize localized refining capabilities.

### 3. EXECUTION

* **Phase I - Bald Peak Mine & Silent Valley Mine (Subterranean Infiltration):** Conduct an overland alpine insertion to penetrate the high-elevation mining networks. Deploy small fireteams to infiltrate the subterranean shafts of **Bald Peak Mine** and **Silent Valley Mine**. Place heavy demolition charges directly onto the primary automated excavation drills and structural support framing to permanently seal off the main mineral veins.
* **Phase II - Mining Control Center (Network Hijack):** Move downslope under strict communication discipline to the **Mining Control Center**. Breach the security gates, neutralize the automated monitoring arrays, and hard-tap the primary database servers. Mirror all industrial logistics logs to identify where existing raw element stockpiles have been shipped across the island.
* **Phase III - Chem-Extraction Complex (Refinery Sabotage):** Execute a high-intensity dynamic assault on the heavily guarded **Chem-Extraction Complex**. Suppress the PMC garrison defending the refining towers. Locate the hazardous chemical processing valves, override the automated safety locks, and trigger a controlled internal thermal overload to ruin the refinement machinery without causing an external environmental breach.
